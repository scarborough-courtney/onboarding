Computing Resources

For more information on all things computing-related at NCEAS you can visit our Computing wiki. All sections of our wiki are also linked here below:

Getting and Staying Connected

  • Email - Learn more about how we use the Gmail web interface and sign up for our listservs and Google groups.

  • Calendaring - At NCEAS we use Google Calendar to schedule our meetings and our meeting rooms. Learn more about our Google calendaring system.

  • Slack - We stay connected for remote collaboration and community exchanges using Slack. Learn more about Slack and sign up for the NCEAS organization.

  • Printing - Instructions for installing and using printers at NCEAS.

  • Working from Home - check out our tools and practices for staying connected remotely.

  • Laptops for Checkout - We have laptops available for temporary check-out for meetings or other limited uses. Contact Thomas in office #321 for more details.
